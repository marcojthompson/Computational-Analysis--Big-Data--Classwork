'''Baron Macabre''' is a fictional character appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]].

==Publication history==
Baron Macabre first appeared in ''[[Jungle Action]]'' #9 (May 1974), and was created by [[Don McGregor]] and [[Gil Kane]].

The character subsequently appears in ''Jungle Action'' #10-11 (July, September 1974), and #17 (September 1975).

==Fictional character biography==
Baron Macabre is a [[Wakanda (Marvel)|Wakandan]] subversive capable of reanimating the dead as zuvembies.  He was an ally of [[Erik Killmonger]] and came into conflict with the [[Black Panther (comics)|Black Panther]].<ref>''Jungle Action'' #9-11 (May, July, September 1974)</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
Baron Macabre was empowered by the Resurrection Altar. He can fire electric blasts.
